Step 1: Spot-Checking with Lighthouse

While coding, we used the built-in **Google Lighthouse** in Chrome. It allowed us to instantly audit individual pages without ever leaving our development environment. It gave us the immediate feedback loop required to catch accessibility debt before it even reached the main branch.

Step 2: Scaling with Unlighthouse

Once the site grew, spot-checking wasn't enough. We deployed **Unlighthouse**—a free and open-source tool that crawls the entire sitemap in parallel. It took the engineering rigor of Lighthouse and applied it to every single URL at once.
